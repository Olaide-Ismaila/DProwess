Courses

Piano

Piano is a versatile and widely used musical instrument, for its ability to play both melody and harmony. Our piano course ranges from beginner to advance level, we have online training, private home training or you can come to our school.

Organ is a unique and complex art form, distinct from piano; it is closely associated with church music expecially christian worship. You can get good training at our online, private or school training

Violin

Violin is a string instrument that is known for is expressive and versatility. It is one of the most important instrument in classical music. Our violin lessons is both virtual physical, join any of our classes

Guitar

Guitar is an amazing musical instrument. In our school, we tutor learners be a lead guitarist or a bass guitarist; You can join us or our unique online learning platforms.

Saxophone

Saxophone is a woodwind musical instrument commonly used in a wide range of musical genres. it is known for its rich, warm tone and ability to produce both smooth sound and bright notes. join our saxophone class and experience transformation in your playing.

Clarinet

Clarinet is a woodwind instrument that is known for its wide and versatile tone, which can be warm and mellow. It consist of cylindrical body with a flare bell at the end. Join our clarinet class now.

Trumpet

Trumpet is a powerful brass musical instrument, known for its bright piercing sound and its role in in various music genres including classical, jazz and pop. Our trumpet class is very detailed and learner-oriented, join us today for an exciting class.

Sight Reading

Sight reading is the ability to read and perform music at first sight, without prior rehearsal or familiiarity with the piece. its a crucial skill for musicians, especialy in ensemble setting, auditions and learning new pieces quickly. Join our classes today.

Music Theory

Music theory is the study of the fundamental elements that make up music and how these elements interact. Understaning music theory helps musicians read write and comprehend music at a deeper level. Join our classes today.
